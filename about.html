    • About

    My name is Cécile. I am a first-year student in BUT MMI.

    Passionate about the digital world and its various facets, I enjoy evolving and being swept away by its numerous innovations, while challenging myself into learning new things.

    The idea for my concept of providing creative spaces and art galleries comes from a deep appreciation for art and a passion for painting. As someone who has learned painting by herself, I have likely encountered the challenges of finding suitable spaces to practice and develop my skills.

    It was a struggle to find places that provide not only the creative space but also the necessary tools and materials. This personal experience has inspired the vision to create a space where artists, regardless of their background or level of experience, can access the resources and support they need to flourish creatively.
